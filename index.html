About

Nem.Os is a personal studio, an anonymous and collective identity, born from someone who has seen design up close, but always from behind.

He has silently signed furniture shown at the Fuorisalone, developed elements for iconic brand spaces, and supported ideas that echoed in museums. But that work often felt like a paradox: the sensation of building meaningless things for those who never have to justify anything.

With Nem.Os, a new trajectory begins: autonomous, silent, radical. A design that no longer asks permission, that doesn't seek the special object, but the right one.
